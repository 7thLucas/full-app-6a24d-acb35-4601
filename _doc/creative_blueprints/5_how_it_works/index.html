How It Works

One gentle loop per chat — then a cozy RPG progression layer that turns wins into confidence.

Situation

The NPC sets a real scene in plain English.

STEP 2

Challenge

Pick the natural Korean phrase — Hangul, romanization, meaning.

STEP 3

Feedback

Warm praise, or a gentle correction — never a punishment.

STEP 4

Reward

+XP, coins, "Phrase unlocked!" — a satisfying pixel pop.

STEP 5

Phrasebook

Saved forever, with mastery that deepens as you review.
