The problem

Beginners learn Korean through flashcards and quiz screens, then freeze the moment they have to actually order a coffee or ask for directions in Seoul. The drills feel like homework, not like life — so confidence never arrives.

✓

The solution

A compact, living pixel-art Hongdae you actually explore. Press E to talk to a barista, a street-food vendor, a busker. Every shop, sign, and NPC teaches a real phrase you need to progress — learning embedded in the world, never a quiz app.
