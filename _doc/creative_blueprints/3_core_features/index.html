Core Features

Learning, woven into a town

For the beginner who just arrived in Hongdae and doesn't speak much Korean yet.

Foreign visitor / exchange student Real beginner
🗺️

Explorable pixel Hongdae

A compact top-down map — subway exit, pedestrian street, cafes, food stalls, busking zone, noraebang alley — that's dense and alive but stays readable.

💬

Press E to talk

Walk up, see the prompt, hit E. A dialogue box opens with a real situation; you choose the right Korean phrase and get friendly feedback.

🧑‍🍳

NPCs with a purpose

Barista, food vendor, student, cashier, busker, noraebang staff — six+ locals, each teaching one practical slice of survival Korean.

🪧

Objects that teach

Menu boards, food carts, subway maps, fridges, photo booths, noraebang signs — eight+ props that unlock vocabulary or a cultural note.

📖

XP, coins & phrasebook

Every chat earns XP, coins, or friendship points and saves the phrase to a persistent phrasebook — small wins that stack into confidence.

🕹️

Cozy RPG feel

WASD/arrow movement, a chibi player, smooth top-down camera, pixel HUD panels, and a warm lo-fi soundscape — original art with a golden-hour Seoul vibe.
